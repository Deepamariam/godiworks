Through thick and thin
To make the music louder, the days brighter, the happiness longer. In this new-era, where life is extremely busy, everyone needs a person to take bad days and turn the into better nights, to help put back the pieces, to nourish, to inculcate better virtues, and above all, to make discover oneself. And here we are, through thick and thin, friend for life.
Life is full of changes, big or small. Nothing stays the same. And everyday take a moment to stop, think and thank for all the blessings in this big world. Feel happy, proud and content. No matter wherever you go or whatever happens, know well that you are gifted.
Do not be afraid to let go. Close your eyes, open your heart, clear your mind. Let go of everything that is weighing you down. New beginnings, new hopes, new concepts. Always make a room for something new. Never be afraid to try something new, because life gets boring when you stay within the limits of what you already know.
The world can seem massive and discouraging, but don't let this stop you from discovering the bliss and beauty. When you make a mistake there are only three things you should ever do about it: admit it, learn from it, and don't repeat it.
